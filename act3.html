My Elevator Design

Elevator is probably one the most efficient way of going to a certain floor much fast and more efficient to us worker because we will no longer have the effort to go toward ladders and waste so much energy in our morning life or even to our everyday lives, so if were to design an interface for 1000 floor elevator it will be presentable to the public and much easier for them to use.

So my first idea for the design is to put a touch screen device where you have to input your designated floor and it will be save in the where it will be save and reset once the elevator reach the proper floor of the user, this will a good help for the user so he/she will avoid a large amount of button inside of the elevator and it will help conserve space for other people to use.

Next is that I will provide a class system in the elevator so the elevator will have a good idea about the person who is riding it, for example a manager has entered in to the elevator he/she will be provided a private card provided by the company or inside of the ID that they have been given to be able to swipe on the screen with the use of NFC (Near Field Communication) or RFID(Radio Frequency Identification) this will help the elevator to go exactly what floor do they want and save the time.

Follow up is for the convenience for those who ride inside of the elevator is that we will provide a classical music or other calming sound that might help them relax inside of the elevator and also different kind of colored ceiling or creative art on each side of the elevator to help them appreciate artists design and ideas of other people, this way we can help them separate from work for a short amount of time.
